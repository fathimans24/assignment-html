And There Was a Light

And There Was Light: Abraham Lincoln and the American Struggle

Description:"The Kite Runner" by Khaled Hosseini is a powerful and poignant novel that explores themes of guilt, redemption, friendship, and the complex nature of human relationships. Set against the backdrop of a changing Afghanistan, the story follows the life of Amir, a young boy from a wealthy family in Kabul, and his complicated friendship with Hassan, the son of his father's servant.
